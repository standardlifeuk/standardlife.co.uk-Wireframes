What is our EasyISA?

It's a stocks and shares ISA that invests in our award winning MyFolio Managed Fund. If you're looking for an easy, tax -efficient way to invest for up to five years or more, this is a good option for you.

Why open an EasyISA?

  • Easy - start investing by making one simple decision

    Choose our MyFolio Managed Funds, and all you have to do is decide on the risk level that's right for you.

  • Easy - your investment will be expertly managed

    Let the experts at Standard Life Investments take care of your investment. They'll actively monitor and manage the performance of the fund and ensure it always meets your chosen level of risk.

  • Easy - manage your account anytime, anywhere

    You can access your money at any time. Login to our online services or use our mobile app.
